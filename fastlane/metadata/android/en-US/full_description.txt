f.Tintra is a simple, fast, and open-source color picker for Android.

Select any image from your device, tap anywhere on it, and instantly get the exact color of that pixel. Copy HEX and RGB values with a single tap and use them in your designs, themes, websites, or applications.

Features

• Pick colors from any image
• Accurate pixel-by-pixel color detection
• Copy HEX color values
• Copy RGB color values
• Material 3 interface
• Dynamic Color support on Android 12+
• Light and dark mode support
• Free and open source

Privacy

f.Tintra works entirely on your device.

• No ads
• No tracking
• No analytics
• No internet connection required
